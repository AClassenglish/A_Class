METHODOLOGY

How Our Lessons Work

Each course follows a clear, structured, and proven 6-step learning process.

1

Initial Assessment

Complete a placement test or consultation to determine your current level, identify strengths/weaknesses, and recommend the best programme.

2

Personalised Study Plan

Your teacher creates a custom study plan based on your level, goals, available preparation time, and target result.

3

Explanation & Guided Practice

The teacher introduces new material, explains important strategies, and demonstrates how to complete tasks correctly.

4

Independent Practice

Students complete exercises, exam tasks, speaking activities, or writing assignments to apply what they have learned.

5

Detailed Feedback

Teachers provide clear corrections on grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, task achievement, accuracy, or exam technique.

6

Progress Monitoring

Results are continuously monitored through homework, practice tasks, progress tests, and mock examinations.
